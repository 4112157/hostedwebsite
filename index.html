Recipes

Recipe snippets from our upcoming cookbook "Cakes to Make you Happy: Recipes passed down from Me, to Myself, to I."

A

APPLE

This apple cake is not only delicious, but easy to make. With it's beautiful layer of poached apple slices, complimented with mixed spices, this apple cake is a tasty way of keeping the doctors away.

ANGEL'S FOOD

A super simple angel's food cake recipe! This is a type of sponge cake that get's its delicate, aerated texture from whipping the egg whites and contains no added fats. This cake is good enough to eat on it's own, but also works well served with berries and cream. Have this cake with a cup of tea, or a cup of soup or a cup of alcohol - whatever makes you happy!

B

BLUEBERRY

Blueberry cakes are one of the easiest cakes to make. Whip up a plain vanilla cake batter and throw in yur blueberries - fresh or frozen, either works well. If using fresh berries, coat them in flour to help prevent them from sinking. You can do the same with frozen berries, but make sure to use them straight out of the freezer to prevent your batter from turning blue.

BANANA

A simple banana cake with a lemon cream cheese icing. When choosing bananas for baking, it is best to use older / overripe bananas because their sugars have had a longer time to develop. This gives the cake a deeper flavour. Too many of you wussies are scared of brown bananas, when those have a carry more flavour than perfect looking bananas. Even if the skin of your banana is completely brown, it doesn't necessarily mean it is rotten - you'll know a rotten banana when it is mushy and sour-smelling. Anyways, top your cake with banana chips or nuts. Or if you're feeling a little bold, top it with freshly sliced bananas and let it oxidize and turn brown and slimy!

CARROT

Carrot cakes look like vomit when you make them, but they DON'T have the same effect when you taste them. This carrot cake is layered with lemon cream cheese icing and topped with chopped nuts. This carrot cake recipe is very versatile, as you can add in just about any nuts and dried fruit that you have handy.

Don't knock it til you try it! This is an original recipe- a honey cake with layers of syrup-soaked eggplant slices. The roughness of the cake and the slipperyness of the eggplant perfectly compliment each other like shoes with socks!

F

FRUITCAKE

Our take on a fruitcake recipe full of candied and dried fruit, nuts, and spices. This cake is quite dry on it's own, so before serving, completely soak your slice in brandy or whiskey. This adds that classic fruitcake flavour, as well as moisture. Consider serving the cake with serveral shots on the side, keeping yourself and your guests extra happy. :D

FLOWER CAKE

Have you ever wanted to be Sid the Sloth? If so, make this cake during springtime so you don't have to settle for the last dandelion of the season! Every element of this cake has flowers in it, from the cake to the icing. This cake is full of flowers for the sake of being full of flowers. We recommend using edible flowers, but at the end of the day, it is up tp you.

G

GLUTEN FREE

We all know that gluten free cakes aren't usually the tastiest- and this recipe proves just that. To make this cake all you need is one litre of water, A loaf of Gerry's of your choosing and your ptsd medecations. Begin by using approximately 250ml of water to wash down your meds. Then, whizz the bread up in a food processor. Or, if you are feeling angsty, pulverise it with your fists. Once you have your breadcrumbs, add enough water to make a workable dough. Press the dough into your lined cake pan, and bake for 15 minutes. Make or buy some meringues to top the cake with and lessen the sad. Now you are ready to serve and turn that coeliac frown upside down!

Delicious and simple cake, with lemons soaked in syrup that they're almost candied. Your cake pan size doesn't really matter, as long as you have enough lemons slices to cover the bottom. What makes our marble cake recipe so great is the inclusion of tiny marbles baked into the batter, making a perfect gift to give to your enemies!
